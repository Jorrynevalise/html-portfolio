The Best Movies According to Jorryn

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Big Hero

This is a cartoon movie about how the main character fights against his enemy with 'the white hero'. The love and the bonding between the main character and his 'robot' are very touching.

人生大事

This is a chinese movie about a man who experienced a lot of struggles in life, but he encountered a girl who lighted up his life, and finally adopted her as his child.

The Princess Diary

A movie about an ordinary girl became the princess of her countries and found her true love. Along the way, she also had breakthrough a lot to finally making a decision to become her country's princess.
